About Us

Google Play Store app data has enormous potential to drive app-making businesses to success. Actionable insights can be drawn for developers to work on and capture the Android market. This is the basis for this Machine Learning Web Application. It takes on important features such as:


  • • Price: Money charged for free non-limited use of the application
  • • Android Version Support: Range of Android Operating Systems in which the app performs optimally
  • • Current Version: App Version after certain number of revises and developments
  • • App Size: Total size of Application that is imposed on users' devices
  • • Category: A general class providing a similar themed service in which the app falls into
  • • Genre: A more specific, niche part of a certain category's market
  • • Price Type: Whether or not the app is free.
  • • Content Rating: The right or ideal age group for the app to be introduced to
  • • Planned Release Date: Relative Month in which app or update is introduced to the market

Developers and Digital Marketers should find the most benefit from this, as it shall allow viewage of statistics, mainly trends that affect app rating the most and focus on it.


You could use the dashboard purposed to present an Exploratory Data Analysis based on the Google Play Store Apps Dataset found on kaggle.
Or, use P-review and see how your existing or even 'under developnent' app should ideally perform in the market post-release.
